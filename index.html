Our Mission

The Division of Institutional Advancement furthers public understanding and recognition of The University of Texas at Arlington and its important mission as a leading research university. We support and build strong relationships with our community, including students, alumni, community members, donors, media, parents, and friends.

Giving

Student success is at the heart of our fundraising efforts, and UTA donors make it possible for talented students and faculty to reach new heights. From groundbreaking research to scholarships that change lives, you can play a significant role in advancing UTA as a model, 21st-century research university.
